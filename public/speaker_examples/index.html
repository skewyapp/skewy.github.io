Improve sound effect

Here are examples on how to improve the sound effect. Meaning being able to play it more quiet - with the same masking effect.

Using quality tape to cover the microphone and if the speaker sits next to it, Skewy’s sound effect is enhanced for the mic while being more silent to its sorroundings.

More sophisticated enhancement can be acheived with an external loudspeaker attached to the mic. The picture with the clothespin is just to highlight the principle. You can use 3D printed clamps with a tiny speaker inserted. I personally use a bumper case with a speaker taped in. With a bit of DIY, you can use usual earphones to build it:
